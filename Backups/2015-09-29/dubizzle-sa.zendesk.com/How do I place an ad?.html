How do I place an ad?

Placing an ad on dubizzle is easy and only takes a few moments of your time.  Follow the steps below to place your ad on dubizzle.com.

  • Click on (place an ad) button

  • Pick a category 

  • Then pick the sub category that match your item

  • Fill all the required blanks

  • Click on (Yalla! Publish my ad)

 

For further questions you may always contact our support team by visiting http://dubai.dubizzle.com/contact/
