How to create an account in OLX?

Creating an account is a simple process which can be initiated by registering your details as shown in the image below. You can also click on the link to register your details Register

 

                       

 

Having an account is more powerful and more efficient to make use of OLX.in! Having an account gives you instant access to the tabs My Account, where you will be able to:

  1. Edit your ads
  2. Remove your ads
  3. Check the response to your ads
  4. View, add, or delete the "observed" ad
  5. Activate your inactive ads, change your account settings (change the e-mail address, password, etc.)

Remember! At any time you can change your password. Just use password change form.

           

After submitting the form, we will send to your e-mail a link to activate your new password - click it to activate the new password.
