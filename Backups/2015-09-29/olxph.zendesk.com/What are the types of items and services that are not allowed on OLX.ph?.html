What are the types of items and services that are not allowed on OLX.ph?

The following items are not allowed from being posted on OLX.ph (list may subject to change without prior notice)

  • CURRENT Philippine Currency, Bills and Coins.
  • Replicas, imitations, bootlegs or copies of original items (i.e. watches, bags, gadgets)

  • Over-the-counter drugs
  • Abortifacient Drugs (promoting abortion) and any form of steroidal drugs
  • Health and beauty products (e.g. slimming coffee, cosmetics make-up with high lead content) and unregistered drugs and/or products
  • Alcohol, Wine and Cigarettes
  • Hazardous, Restricted, and Perishable Items
  • Hazardous or harmful Chemical Products (e.g. insecticides, pesticides, etc.)
  • Fireworks, Explosives and Explosive Substances
  • Telecom company modems/routers issued as part of service contracts

You cannot also post 'Looking for', 'Wanted to buy' and 'Swap only' ads.

You cannot also post ads bearing the word 'SOLD' on the title and/or description for the sake of showing that the item is SOLD and without the intention of actually selling the item.

You will receive a private message and e-mail notification from us in case your ad gets denied for having one or more of the items listed above.
