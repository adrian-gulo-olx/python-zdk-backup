For the following categories, you’ll be charged based on the price of the item that you are selling.

  • Mobile Phones and Tablets
  • Computers
  • Consumer Electronics
  • Clothing and Accessories
  • Books, Sports and Hobbies
  • Baby Stuff and Toys
  • Automotive Car Parts and Accessories
  • Motorcycle Parts and Accessories

 

If your item is worth PHP 500.00 and below, you’ll only be charged a flat fee of PHP 10.00 when posting your additional ad.
