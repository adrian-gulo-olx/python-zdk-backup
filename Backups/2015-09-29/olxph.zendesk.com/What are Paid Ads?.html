What are Paid Ads?

Paid Ads are ads that a user may post in excess of his free active ad allocation which can be posted by purchasing Ad Slots. You can avail of these slots by using your OLX Gold Credits or paying directly via Credit Card or PayPal.

What are Ad Slots?

Ad Slots are what users need to purchase if they want to sell more items but have consumed their free ads allocation in a particular category. The number of free ads and cost of paid Ad Slots vary per category.

How does one avail of paid ads?

1. When you are about to submit an ad that exceeds the free active ad limit, you will be redirected to the Paid Limits Paywall page.

If you wish to proceed, you may simply click ‘Continue posting this ad for PHP 100.00’.

The paywall page also allows users to deactivate one of their currently active ads in order to avoid paying posting an additional ad.

You may also read more about paid ad slots through a link provided in the paywall page.

2. After clicking ‘Continue posting this ad for PHP 100’ button, you will be redirected to the ‘Purchase Ad Slot’ page.

You may now select your preferred payment option. You may either have it deducted from your OLX Gold Credits, or pay directly using your Credit Card or Paypal account. After selecting a payment method, click the ‘Pay’ button.

This page shows you all the details of the ad slot you will be paying for.

If you don’t proceed in purchasing the ad slot, the ad will still be available in the Pending Ads section of your account.

3. After the payment is confirmed, you will be redirected to the confirmation page.
