What are the advantages of having a Primary contact number?

 

1. A user no longer need to recall his OLX.ph username or his email address when logging in to the site, as the Primary Contact no. serves as the main account reference.

2. When posting an ad. You can receive the notification messages via SMS and will no longer need an email address

  • a. Ad posting status
  • b. If your ad has violations
  • c. When your ad is copied and deleted as new
  • d. When someone just viewed your ad for the first time
