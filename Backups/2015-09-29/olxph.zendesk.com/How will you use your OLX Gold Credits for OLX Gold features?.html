How will you use your OLX Gold Credits for OLX Gold features?

You may enjoy the advantages of OLX Gold features for your advertisement using OLX Gold by following these steps:

  1. Login your OLX.ph account.
  2. Once logged in, you will be redirected on your ‘My Account’ page.
  3. Click ‘Featured Ad’ at the right side of the ad you want to add exposure to.
  4. You will be redirected to the ‘Promote Ad’ page.
  5. Choose the preferred package/feature that you want to apply to your ad.

Available packages:

  • Good for 30 days (Sponsored and Highlighted for 30 days, Automatic Repost every 7 days)
  • Good for 7 days (Sponsored and Highlighted for 7 days)

Available custom promotional features:

  • Sponsor Ad (up to 28 days, up to 5 bids)
  • Highlight Ad (up to 28 days)
  1. Choose your preferred payment option. You may either use your OLX Gold credits or pay through Credit Card or Paypal.
  2. Once you’ve selected your payment option, click ‘Buy Feature’ to apply the feature to your ad.
