My Favorites

If you are interested in different ads and you want to save them to check them later, you can mark them as "Favorites"

Follow these steps to use it:

  • Login to your account
  • Search for the product you are interested and click on the title of the ad
  • At the bottom of the add, click on "Add to favorites" (see below)

  • Now, go to My Favorites. You can find this option at the top right of your window

         

  • Find a list with all the ads you marked. From here you will also be able to Contact the Seller

Remember! "My Favorites" section is only available for register users. If you don't have an account, create one!
