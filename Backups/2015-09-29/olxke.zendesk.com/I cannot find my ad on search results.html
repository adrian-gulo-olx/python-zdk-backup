I cannot find my ad on search results

Please follow these steps; If you cannot find your ad on the search results:

  • Cross check the spelling of your search. Remember to add specific words or the exact title of your ad.
  • Results always show all active ads. If your ad does not appear, check  your account the status of your ad. If the ad has expired, please, post it again
  • In case your ad was rejected, please review our Posting Rules, and the list of the Prohibited items before you edit it. You will receive an email with the reason of the rejection, and a link to edit your ad directly.

If you still have questions, please feel free to Contact Us. We will reply to you as soon as possible.
