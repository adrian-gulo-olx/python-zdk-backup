Do I need to register to use OLX?

Yes to post on OLX you need to register. If you posted without registering click forgot password.

To edit your ad, check your conversations to buyers and sellers, and delete your ads, you need to create an account.

Having an account is more powerful and more efficient to make use of OLX!

You can create your account by clicking here. The only info we will request is your email and password.

                        

 

After submitting the form, we will send to your e-mail a link to activate your account.
