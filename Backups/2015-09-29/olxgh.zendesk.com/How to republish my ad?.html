How to republish my ad?

The republish option is only available for the ads you manually removed from your account.

If you want to republish your ad:

  • Go to the OLX homepage
  • Click on "Login" and enter your email and password
  • Click on “My ads”. You can find this option at the upper right section of the website
  • Click on the Republish button, located at the right of your removed ads
  • Done! Your ad will automatically republish.
