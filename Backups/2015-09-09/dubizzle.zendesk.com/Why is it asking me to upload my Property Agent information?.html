Why is it asking me to upload my Property Agent information?

To ensure dubizzle is a healthy and safe property marketplace for our users, we need to verify that you are legally allowed to exercise real estate brokerage activities before you can list properties on our website.

To list properties in Dubai, you must upload a copy of your valid RERA broker card.


To list properties in other Emirates, you must upload a copy of your valid Visa showing your current employer name AND your employer's trade license that clearly states that your company is allowed to exercise real estate brokerage activities.



These documents are only requested from you once until your RERA card or other documents expire, in which case we will ask you to upload your new RERA card or other documents.

Once you upload the requested documents, our customer support will review their validity within 24 hours and approve your account. You can then immediately start listing properties on dubizzle.
