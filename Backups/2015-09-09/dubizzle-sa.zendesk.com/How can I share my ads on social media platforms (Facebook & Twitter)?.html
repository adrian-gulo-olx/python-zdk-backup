How can I share my ads on social media platforms (Facebook & Twitter)?

You can share your ad with your friends on social media (Facebook & Twitter) by:

  • clicking on the social media buttons under your ad.

 

  • To share through Facebook, log in to your account through the window below and click share.

 

               

 

  • To share through Twitter, Log in to your account Through the window below and click "sign in & tweet".
