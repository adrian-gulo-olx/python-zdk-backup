Troubleshooting Your App

Here are the basic troubleshooting steps you can do should your app run anything but smoothly. You may try these steps at your discretion.

  1. Establish a strong and stable connection
  2. Tests the app using other devices and connections (e.g. other wifi hotspots/mobile data) if possible
  3. Make sure your device has sufficient memory
  4. Close all other running apps to help improve your device's performance
  5. Relaunch the app by closing and opening it again
  6. Clear the app's cache from your device's app manager/settings
  7. Update to the latest version if you haven't done so yet
  8. Reinstall the app

Should none of the steps above do the trick, contact our Support Team to report the issue by sending an email to support@olx.ph. Indicate your device's brand/model, OS version, connection, and app version in your email.
