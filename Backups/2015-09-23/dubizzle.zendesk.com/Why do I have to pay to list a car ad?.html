Why do I have to pay to list a car ad?

In order to provide and maintain the biggest open marketplace for car lovers in the UAE, we need to keep investing in innovative new features and services that improve user experience.

Listing an ad on dubizzle motors section has historically been free for users. However, as of December 3rd 2014 dubizzle will charge a fee of AED 199.

This change is part of our efforts to maintain and grow the UAE’s biggest open marketplace in the Middle East.
