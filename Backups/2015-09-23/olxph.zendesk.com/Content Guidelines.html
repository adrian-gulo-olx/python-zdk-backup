Content Guidelines

You must ensure that all content you post on our site must comply with these rules and shall not contain:

  1. Adult content or those of a sexual nature, or any material which the dominant theme appeals to prurient interest as a whole and satisfies only the craving for gratuitous sex, including the following:
  • Nudity meant simply to titillate and has little aesthetic value;
  • Portrayal of sexual activity;
  • Images of the genitalia; and,
  • Any other material, which in our opinion, may be considered as adult content or sexual in nature.
  1. Material depicting excessive violence, such as that which:
  • Glorifies or glamorizes violence;
  • Satisfies the craving for gratuitous violence;
  • Contains detailed or prolonged images of other kinds of violence;
  • Glorifies criminals or endorses criminal behavior; and,
  • Any other material, which in our opinion, is offensive in nature or depicts excessive violence.
  1. Foul, offensive, or improper language;
  2. Racist, hateful, inflammatory or flaming comments;
  3. Reference to any illegal material or activity;
  4. Products or services that are prohibited or illegal;
  5. Confusing, deceptive or misleading information; or,
  6. Material which infringes the intellectual property rights of third parties.
