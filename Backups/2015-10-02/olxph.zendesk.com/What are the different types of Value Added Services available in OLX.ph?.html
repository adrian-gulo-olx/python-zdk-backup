What are the different types of Value Added Services available in OLX.ph?

Listed below are the different Value Added Services that can be purchased to promote a user’s ad:

Packages

For Buy and Sell Categories

    • 7-day Package (PHP199)
      • 7 days Sponsor Ads
      • 7 days Highlight Ad
    • 30-day Package (PHP599)
      • 30 days Sponsor Ads
      • 30 days Highlight

For Cars, Real Estate, Services, Jobs, and Business and Earning Opportunities

    • 7-day Package (PHP299)
      • 7 days Sponsor Ads
      • 7 days Highlight Ad
    • 30-day Package (PHP699)
      • 30 days Sponsor Ads
      • 30 days Highlight

Stand-alone Value Added Services

    • Sponsored Ads
    • Highlight Ad
    • Express Refresh
    • Scheduled Refresh

The cost of each Stand-alone VAS varies depending on the duration and the category the ad is posted in. You can check the cost for your advertisements by clicking the  button.
