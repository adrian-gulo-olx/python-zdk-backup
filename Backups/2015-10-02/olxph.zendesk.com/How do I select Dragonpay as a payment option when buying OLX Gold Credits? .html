How do I select Dragonpay as a payment option when buying OLX Gold Credits?

  1. Buy OLX Gold as you regularly do.
  2. (No need to look for the Dragonpay Logo as a payment tile) you may select either ATM/Debit Card, GCash, or Other Payment Centers as the payment tiles.

  1. Make sure that you agree to OLX Terms and Conditions and Click ‘Continue’.

You will be redirected to the Dragonpay payment portal.

  1. Select which payment facility you want to use, agree to Dragonpay’s terms and conditions and click ‘Continue’.

  1. Input your email address to receive the payment instructions and click the ‘ Send Instructions via Email’ button

  1. Follow instructions found in the email (Pay, Validate Payment, Wait for payment confirmation).

Make sure that you’ll receive the payment confirmation from Dragonpay.

After your payment has been confirmed, your purchased OLX Gold credit will reflect on your balance.
