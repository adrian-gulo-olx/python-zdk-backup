What is the Express Refresh feature?

The Express Refresh feature can be used to boost your ad’s duration and exposure as it bumps up your ad at the top of our search results as if it were a new listing. You can easily restart your ad’s posting date, moving its expiration 28 days after it has been refreshed.

The Express Refresh is a helpful and affordable way for you to get your items sold, without having to worry about your ad being buried beneath newer ads.
