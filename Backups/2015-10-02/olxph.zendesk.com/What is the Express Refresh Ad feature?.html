What is the Express Refresh Ad feature?

The Refresh feature has been optimized and is now known as Express Refresh. As its name implies, this gives you the ability to instantly bump up and renew the lifetime of your ads whenever you need to--yes, it is now available immediately after posting an ad! You no longer need to wait for 7 days to use this feature unlike before!

Will the Express Refresh feature require OLX Gold Credits?

Yes, the new and improved Express Refresh feature requires a minimal fee to ensure quality listings on the website, giving ad owners the advantage to receive more exposure instantly at an extended time which, therefore, helps increase opportunities and engagements from potential buyers.
