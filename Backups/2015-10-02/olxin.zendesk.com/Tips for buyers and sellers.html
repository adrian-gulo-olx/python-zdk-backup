Tips for buyers and sellers

Some good practices which allows for effective and safe use of OLX.in

Seller

  1.  Add good quality photos and accurately describe the item you are selling.
  2.  Provide alternative ways of contacting you like chat, mobile number etc.
  3.  Price your item attractively, and you are likely to get more people interested  in your item and service.
  4.  Be polite in your dealings with interested buyers.
  5.  Always meet in person - you earn the trust of buyers.

Buyer

  1.  Use proper search and filters to find exactly what you're looking for.
  2.  Agree on exact details of the transaction with the seller, ask for additional  photos if necessary.
  3.  Respect each others time, be polite in your dealings and transactions.
  4.  Make transactions in person, please do not transfer money in advance.
