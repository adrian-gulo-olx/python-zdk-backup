How to edit my Ad?

Its easy! Choose one of three ways:

  1. Method 1

    • Step 1 - Go to the page of your advertisement
    • Step 2 - Click the "Edit" link
    • Step 3 - You will be taken to a form where you can make changes

  2. Method 2
    Do you have an account on olx.com.pk ?

    • Step 1 - Login to your account
    • Step 2 - Go to the tab "Ads"
    • Step 3 - Go to the "Active ads"
    • Step 4 - Select the ad you want to edit and click the link "Edit"
    • Step 5 - You will be taken to a form where you can make changes

      

  3. Method 3
    Do not have an account olx.com.pk?

    • Step 1 - Check the email "Manage your Ad" which we sent to you after you have posted ads
    • Step 2 - In the body of the message click on "Edit" link
    • Step 3 - You will be taken to a form where you can make changes
