One Time Password ( OTP)

To help our user's we have started OTP (One time Password) for mobile verification. This makes you verified phone user and helps you getting more eyeballs and views to your ad.

What is OTP:-

A one-time password (OTP) is a password that is valid for only one login session or transaction, on a computer system or other digital device.

 

What should i do if i do not receive my OTP via SMS?

Please provide correct mobile number so that new OTP is sent to your registered mobile number. In case you still didn't receive OTP, check for network coverage. Poor network coverage may delay OTP delivery to your phone inbox.
