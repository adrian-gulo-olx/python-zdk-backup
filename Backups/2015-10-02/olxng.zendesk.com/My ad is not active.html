Once you post an ad on OLX; you will receive an email notification immediately. The notification informs you of the status of the ad which is usually pending prior to it being approved or rejected. 

Users also have the options of checking the status of their ads on the My Ads tab which is located on the upper right hand corner.

In the case your ad was rejected; you will receive an email with the reason of the rejection; please review our Posting Rules, and the list of the Prohibited items on OLX and edit your ad accordingly. 

If you still have questions, please feel free to Contact Us. We will reply to you as soon as possible.
