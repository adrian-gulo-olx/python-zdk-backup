Where can I find my messages?

To access to the messages you sent or received, you need to be a register user.

If you don't have an account, register here. You will see all the ads you posted with the same email address, and the messages you sent and received.

To access to your inbox, follow these steps:

  • Login to your account
  • Go to Messages. You can find this option at the top right of your window

  • Find a list with all your conversations
