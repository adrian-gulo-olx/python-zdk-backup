How can you buy OLX Gold Credits?

How much is OLX Gold Credit?

1 OLX Gold Credit = PHP1.00

To purchase OLX Gold, follow these steps:

  1. Login your OLX.ph account.
  2. Once logged in, you will be redirected to your ‘My Account’ page. Click the ‘Manage OLX Gold’ tab.
  3. Beside your current amount of OLX Gold, click ‘Buy OLX Gold Credits’.
  4. You will be redirected to the top up page of OLX Gold.
  • Select how much OLX Gold you want to buy. You may choose from the following amount:
    • PHP100.00
    • PHP200.00
    • PHP500.00
    • PHP1,000.00
    • PHP5,000.00
    • PHP10,000.00
    • PHP30,000.00
    • PHP50,000.00
  • Select your preferred payment option. You may pay through:
    • Real-time Payment Options
      • Credit Card
      • Paypal
    • Over the Counter Payment Options
      • BDO
      • Cebuana Lhuiller
      • LBC
      • 7-Eleven
  • Make sure to read and accept the Terms and Conditions.
  • Once done, click ‘Continue’.
  1. When you use the real-time payment option such as PayPal, you will be redirected to the payment option’s payment portal. When you use the over the counter payment option such as LBC, you will be redirected to the OTC Payment Transactions Page.
  2. To check if the top up has been transferred as an OLX Gold Credit, check your credits balance found in the ‘Manage OLX Gold’ page.

Is there a limit to how much OLX Gold I can purchase?

None. You can purchase OLX Gold Credits continuously without limitations. However, you can only make up to 10 pending/unpaid payment transactions.

On the other hand, if you’re using Credit Card or Paypal payment option, you can only purchase up to PHP 10,000 worth of OLX Gold Credits per month.
