Why did my ad disappear? How long does my ad stay on the website?

Please note that your ad will stay on the site until you delete it, or until it expires.

The length that a listing stays on the website before it expires may vary from city to city.

Currently, however, below is a list of the minimum amount of time an ad stays on the site per section:

Classifieds: 30 days
Motors<used cars : 6 months
Property for Sale: 60 days
Property for Rent: 30 days
Jobs: 14 days (free)
Community: 30 days
