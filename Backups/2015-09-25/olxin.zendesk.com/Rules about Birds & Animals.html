Rules about Birds & Animals

 

    1. In birds, we only allow Chickens, Roosters, Chicks, Geese, Emus, Pigeons, Turkeys & Ducks.
    2. In Animals, we only allow Dogs, Cats, Fishes, Bovine, Pigs, Equines, Rabbits, Hamsters, Guinea Pigs & Goats.
    3. Birds and animals used for fighting or racing is strictly prohibited.
    4. OLX only allows ads selling Meat & Eggs of the animals/birds listed above with an exception of Cow meat.
    5. We do not allow animals & birds which fall under the restricted category as per THE INDIAN WILDLIFE (PROTECTION) ACT 1972 & WORLD WILDLIFE FEDERATION.
