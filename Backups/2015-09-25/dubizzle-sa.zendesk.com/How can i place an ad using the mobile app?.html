How can i place an ad using the mobile app?

To place an ad using the mobile app, first make sure you are logged in, if you don't have an account you can click here to know how to create one.

  • Once you log in click on "Place an Ad"

  • Choose the category that your ad fits into

  • Then choose the sub-category for your ad

  • Enter a title for your ad, and add pictures for what you are selling, then click next.

  • Fill in all the information needed and then click on "Yalla! Publish my ad"
