How can I delete My Account?

Only registered users can delete their account.

We request you to please follow below steps to delete your account with us:

  1. Go to OLX homepage
  2. Enter "My Account" using your email id and password.
  3. Click on "Settings"
  4. Click "Delete account"
  5. Click on "Delete Account" appearing in red, a prompt pop-up will appear, confirm it again.

Done, your account/profile is deleted.

If you wish to use our services in future, do contact us back, we would be happy to have you with us.
