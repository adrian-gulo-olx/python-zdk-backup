Where can I find my messages?

Every time a buyer sends a message through your ad using the "Email Seller" field, you will receive a notification at the email account you used.

You will also receive messages from interested buyers through your Account on OLX. You can use "My Account" to see all messages sent by interested buyers for your active ads.

If you are a registered user, just follow these simple steps:

  1. Click on “My Account” on the OLX homepage and enter your username and password.
  2. A notification will appear on the screen under "Messages".
  3. To respond: Select the message, write your response in the available field and click “Reply”
  4. Make sure to answer any buyer or seller questions with accurate information. You may include a phone number or other contact information in these messages to streamline the transaction.

If you haven't registered with OLX, you will get an email on your personal mailbox, you just need to click on the button "See the message and reply".

In this way you are redirected to your unregistered account and can check messages & reply to them too.
