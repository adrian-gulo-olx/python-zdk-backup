What are the payment options available in purchasing OLX Gold Credits?

BDO - things to secure and take note of:

  • Payment Slip
  • Subscriber’s Account Number / Reference Number
  • Company Name: OLX Philippines (You can still use Sulit.com.ph)

Cebuana Lhuillier - things to secure and take note of:

  • Transaction Reference Number
  • Registered Email Address for OLX.ph Account
  • Institution: OLX Philippines (You can still use Sulit.com.ph)

LBC - things to secure and take note of:

  • Transaction Reference Number
  • Registered Email Address for OLX.ph Account
  • Institution: OLX Philippines (You can still use Sulit.com.ph)

7-Eleven - things to secure and take note of:

  • Amount
  • Transaction Reference Number
  • Institution: OLX Philippines (You can still use Sulit.com.ph)

Credit Card - things to secure and take note of:

  • Card Number
  • Expiry Date
  • Security Code
  • 3D Secure Code (If applicable)

 PayPal - automated

  • Complete payment details and procedure will be displayed after submission of amount of OLX Gold to buy.

How long will it take before my payment gets processed?

For payments that require processing (non-automated), pending OLX Gold Credit payments will be processed within 48 hours (2 business days) from submission, excluding on weekends and holidays.

Automated options such as BDO, LBC, Cebuana Lhuiller, and 7/11 payments are processed within two (2) business days.

For real-time OLX Gold credits, use these payment options:

  • Paypal
  • Credit Card

NOTE: Weekends and Holidays are not counted as BUSINESS DAYS. OLX Philippines' business days are from Monday-Friday only.
