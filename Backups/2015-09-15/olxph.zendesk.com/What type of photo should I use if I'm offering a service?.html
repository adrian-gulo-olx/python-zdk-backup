What type of photo should I use if I'm offering a service?

If possible, we recommend using your company/business logo BUT you also may use event photos, or photos of your shop/office for your ad. Alternatively, you may also upload plain text photos that generally indicates the type of service you're offering. Please take note that photos displaying nudity, violence and anything related will automatically be disapproved.
