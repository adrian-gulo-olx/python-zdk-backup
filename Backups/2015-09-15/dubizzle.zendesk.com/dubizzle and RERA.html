dubizzle and RERA

dubizzle is dedicated to helping RERA (Dubai’s Real Estate Regulatory Agency) enforce its rules and regulations.

As all property advertisements in Dubai must follow certain regulations, particularly that the person or company that advertises the property is validly licensed by RERA to do so, we will be sharing this information with them going forward.


It is critical that all advertisers enter in their correct information, as RERA will be notifying us to which accounts are invalid, and we will be required to de-activate those accounts.


Should you have any questions about RERA advertising policies, please contact them directly at http://www.dubailand.gov.ae/



If you need further assistance, you may always contact our support team by visiting http://dubai.dubizzle.com/contact/
