I have already deleted my account, but why I can't register using the same email address?

Please note that even if you had already deleted your account, the email address will remain registered in our database, therefore it can no longer be used for a new registration or email address change.

We suggest that you use a different email address OR we could reactivate the above mentioned account if you wish, just send a request to our customer support team by visiting http://dubai.dubizzle.com/contact/
