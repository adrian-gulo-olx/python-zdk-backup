Our Service

OLX Philippines endeavors to be a family-safe website. To ensure this, we ask everyone to observe our rules and follow our guidelines.

OLX Philippines, as a community-based system, aims for a "self-moderated" community where members are encouraged to moderate their own posts and also allowed to help others moderate theirs. This way, it will be easier to manage a continually growing community. The members manage the community and administrators. Any member is encouraged to report anything they believe is a violation of these rules and policies.

Necessarily, this includes these Terms and Conditions, Responsibilities of all Users and Members, Content Guidelines, Posting Classified Guidelines, OLX Gold Credits Terms and Conditions, and all other policies found in our Policies Page.
