How do I modify my email address?

If you are a registered user and would like to modify your e-mail address,Â click here.

To change your email address, simply follow the steps below:

  1. Go to the OLX
  2. Enter "My Account" using your email address and password.
  3. Click on "Settings"
  4. Now, click on "Change Email"
  5. Enter your new email address. Once you set the new email address, you will receive an e-mail to reactivate.
  6. Click on Activate to complete the process.
  7. All set! Your email has been changed.
