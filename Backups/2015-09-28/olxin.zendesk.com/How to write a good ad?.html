How to write a good ad?

Do not make life difficult! Incomplete description receives phone calls and emails asking for more details. Describe the product/item you want to sell in more detail.

Place such advertisement where users would like to respond without emotional content and vulgarity. Check the spelling and do not write with CAPS LOCK.

              

                  

 

Clear Title: This should explain the item or service you are selling in 15 characters or less.

Appropriate category: Choose the best suitable category for your item or service that you are offering.

Correct Price: We suggest you research the market value of your item by visiting the respective category on our site and price it accordingly. The price should reflect the proper value of your item. It should not be too low or too high.

Detailed description: This is the story of your item or service. How long has it been used? What condition is it in? Are there any additional accessories included? What areas do you offer services in? Help buyers choose you with a detailed description. Usually about 2-3 sentences of quality information will be enough. Minimum 20 characters are required and maximum of 5000.

Clear Photos: Make sure to include at least two pictures in your ad. They should be clear images that are of the actual product you are selling and should not contain links to other sites. You can have up to 8 pictures of max 5 MB each. Accepted formats are .jpg, .jpeg & .png, also ensure the item is properly visible from multiple angles.
