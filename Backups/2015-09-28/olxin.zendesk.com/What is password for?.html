What is password for?

Having an account is more powerful and more efficient to make use of OLX.in! Having an account gives you instant access to tabs in My Ads, where you will be able to:

  1. Edit your ads
  2. Remove your ads
  3. Check the response to your ads
  4. View, add, or delete the "observed" ad
  5. Activate your deleted ads
  6. Change your account settings (change the e-mail address, password, etc.)

Click to create an account.

Remember! At any time you can change your password. Just use forgot password form.
