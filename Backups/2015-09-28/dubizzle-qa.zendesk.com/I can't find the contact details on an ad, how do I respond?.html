I can't find the contact details on an ad, how do I respond?

Not all users choose to make their contact details public on their ad. If the ad does not have contact details listed in the description, you can send a message directly to their email by clicking the  "Send Reply" button on the right of each ad.

Fill out the information, type your message and press send. Your message will immediately be sent directly to the email address of the user who listed the ad.
