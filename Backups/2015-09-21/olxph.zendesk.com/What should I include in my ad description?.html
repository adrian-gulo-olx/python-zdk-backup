What should I include in my ad description?

Here are some important details to include in your ad description:

  • Brand, model, size, and other specifications
  • How old is your item?
  • Is your price negotiable?
  • What is your RFS (reason for selling)?
  • Where is your preferred meet-up?

Users must put their mobile number in their profile and NOT in the ad description or title. This lets interested buyers contact you directly through the Call and SMS features available on all OLX ads. Alternatively, users may also use the Private Message (PM) option to get in touch with you.
