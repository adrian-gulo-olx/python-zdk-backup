What will happen to my ads which are currently applied with VAS?

VAS applied to your ad will still follow its paid/original expiration date

 

For Sponsored Ads, the equivalent bid will be rounded off to the nearest 10th.

Example: Sponsored Ad Bid (May 25 and earlier) : 45 bid per day

                 Sponsored Ad Bid (May 26 onwards) : 50 bid per day
