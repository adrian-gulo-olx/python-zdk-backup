What does it mean when the “Ooops! You’ve reached the limit of free ads in this category” page appears?

If you receive this system message, this simply means that you have reached the maximum number of ads that you can post for free in whichever category you're posting in. Advertisements published after consuming the free slots in a particular category will require the use of OLX Gold Credits to avail of paid ad slots.
