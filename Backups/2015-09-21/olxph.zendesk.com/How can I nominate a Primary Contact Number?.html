How can I nominate a Primary Contact Number?

You can nominate a Primary Contact number through any of the following:

1. If you do not have an existing OLX.ph account and will post an ad for the first time using your mobile number, OLX.ph will create a user account for you and your listed contact number will be your Primary Contact number.

2. If you already have an existing OLX.ph account and wish to nominate a Primary account number, go to your 'Update Profile' page (https://www.olx.ph/index.php/update+profile) by clicking on ‘Edit Profile’ under your username in the member menu while logged in.
You should check your mobile phone for a confirmation SMS coming from OLX.ph (yes, it's a word and not from a 2366 or any 4-digit number). You can either click this link directly from your smartphone or by typing the link in a browser.

3. You can also directly nominate a Primary contact number by registering to our site by using your mobile number.
