How do I place an ad?

Placing an ad on dubizzle is easy and only takes a few moments of your time. Follow the steps below to place your ad on dubizzle.com.

Once you're logged in, Click on the "Place Your Ad" button in the top right corner of the page.

 

  • Once you're on the Category page, select the category of the item you wish to advertise.

  •  In the sub-category, choose the type of item you wish the advertise 

  • Identify in which type of subcategory it falls.

  • Fill in the details about your ad.

  • To preview your ad click on "Check it live". If you wish to add more information, click on "Edit your ad"

However, First time users will be asked to send an SMS to activate their ad/account.

 

For further questions you may always contact our support team by visiting http://dubai.dubizzle.com/contact/
