How to delete my Ad?

Its easy! Choose one of two ways.

  1. Method 1
    Do you have an account in OLX.in?

    • Step 1 - Log in to the site
    • Step 2 - Go to the "Ads" tab
    • Step 3 - Go to the "Active ads"
    • Step 4 - Select the ad you want to remove and click on the link: "Deactivate Ad" (located under the title of the ad).

       

  2. Method 2
    Do not have an account in OLX.in?

    • Step 1 - Check the email "Manage you Ad", which we send to you after you have posted the ad.
    • Step 2 - In the body of the message, click "Deactivate Ad".

Removed ads will be moved to the "Inactive" tab. You can activate them again whenever you want. Just click on the button "Activate" at the advertisement that you want to restore.
