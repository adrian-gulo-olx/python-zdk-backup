There is NO Photo in AD & the Photo is unsuitable

Photo posting rules on dubizzle are listed below. Please follow them to avoid getting your AD rejected:
• Photo should be of the product being sold
• Photo should be real and not a stock photo from Internet
• Photo should be clear and un-rotated
• Photo should be of product only, with no persons in photo
• Photo should not contain links, emails or logos
• Photo should not be of any illegal or indecent nature.
• Photo should not contain more than one time of the same product
