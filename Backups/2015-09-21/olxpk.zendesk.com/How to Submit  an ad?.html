How to Submit an ad?

Adding ads has never been so easy! Just click on the button Submit an Ad which is located on the main page.

            

                                   

You will be redirected to our posting form. Fill it.

  1. Enter the title, so that based on the title, we can predict the appropriate category to which the ad would belong to.

  2. Select main category and subcategory.

  3. Enter the price, Make sure it reflects the condition of the issuing item.

  4. Describe your ad, Remember! The more detailed the description will be, the greater the chance for a sale. Here are some of our suggestions.

  5. Add images, Thanks to them, you can gain up to 3 times more responses by adding images! You can add a maximum of 8 images into one advertisement. More information about adding images can be found here

  6. Give the contact details for the user. Provide all relevant information requested under contact details field.

  7. Enter a postal code or area, Enter the postal code of the area from where you are offering the ad or service. Don't forget to add city name. It will help you fetch more views.

If you are not a registered user, activate the ad by clicking the link that was sent to you by e-mail. Remember! Registered users can add classifieds without activation from email! Find out why you should set up an account.

Ad will be visible on the website immediately after quick verification by our system.
