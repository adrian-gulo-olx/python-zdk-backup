I cannot access "My Account" section

This could be because account was either suspended or blocked due to breach of policy and terms of use. In case above reason doesn’t hold true please follow below steps to access your account:

  1. Go to the OLX homepage
  2. Click on Forgot Password
  3. Click on change password link on the activation e-mail
  4. Change the Password

You should be now able to access "My Account". IN case you are still facing problem accessing your account, please contact us and we will be more than happy to assist you.
