Managing my ads in "My account"

Ads tab is used to manage listings - both the active and inactive.

How to access bookmarks from My Account? Just go to register page and create an account. Check out the use of an account.

Generally, there are two tabs: Active (Advertisements that are visible on the website) and Inactive (Ads that have been expired or made inactive and are not visible on the site).

           

Ads list in Active tab contains the following data:

  • Posted date
  • The number of responses given to ad
  • Additional Options
  • Preview, edit, remove
  • If you have entered phone number, Skype etc., you'll see the number of views of those numbers. Remember! It's just the number of views, not the number of calls made.
