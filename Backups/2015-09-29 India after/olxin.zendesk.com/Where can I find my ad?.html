Where can I find my ad?

Once your ad is live, you will get a notification email stating that your ad is live on OLX.

In the case your ad is rejected, it may have violated our posting rules. You will also get an email giving a reason for the rejection with a link to edit your ad.

You can keep track of your ads by logging in to your OLX account and clicking on My ads. Here you will see the status of your ads i.e pending, approved or rejected, with options to edit and delete them.
