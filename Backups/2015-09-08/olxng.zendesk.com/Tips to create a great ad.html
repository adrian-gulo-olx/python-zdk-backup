Tips to create a great ad

It is very easy to place an ad on OLX, but you would also like to sell your item quickly and for a good price. Therefore it is important you create a good, attractive ad that appeals to buyers.

  • You must register for an OLX account by providing your basic contact information. If you don't have an account, create one!

                                    

  • Click on the “Sell your item” button   
  • Choose an attractive title and include the name of the item. Avoid characters like %, #,!, @, Etc. And CAPITAL LETTERS.
  • Be very descriptive and concise with the item you're selling. Remember: a great description attracts more buyers to your ad.
  • Upload clear and actual pictures of the product (Pictures from the internet are prohibited). A picture says more than a  Remember great pictures get 5 times more views!

                           

  • The pricing of the item must be reasonable and competitive.
