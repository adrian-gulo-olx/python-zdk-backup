How to edit my ad

Users have the option of editing their ad after it has gone live on the website.

To edit your ad:

  • Go to the OLX homepage. 
  • Click on "Login" and enter your email and password
  • Click on “My ads”. You can find this option at the upper right section of the website
  • Click on the Edit button, located at the right of your ads
  • Edit your ad and click Post


Another option is to click on the “Edit your ad” link on the email notification sent to you when you post your ad, and make the necessary corrections.
