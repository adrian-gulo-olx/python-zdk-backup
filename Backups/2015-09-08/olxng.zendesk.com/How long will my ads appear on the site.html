How long will my ads appear on the site?

  • All ads remain on the OLX site for 90 days before it automatically gets deleted.

                                

  • The seller also has the option of deleting the ad after the product or service has been sold.

                        

  • If you have not sold your item after 90 days; you can repost it with more detailed information regarding the product or service, better image(s) and a competitive price.
