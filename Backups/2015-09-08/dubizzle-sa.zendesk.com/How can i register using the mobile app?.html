How can i register using the mobile app?

To register using the mobile app you can follow the steps below:

  • Open the app and click on "You"

  • Click on "Login / Register"

  • You Can sign in with facebook or you can click on "Don't have an account? Register"

  • Enter your email and a password and click on the green register button.

Once you are done you can now place ads on dubizzle.
