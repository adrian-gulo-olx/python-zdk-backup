How do I edit/delete my ads?

From your "Account" drop down menu, choose "My Ads" page you can edit and/or delete any or your ads on dubizzle.com.

To visit your "My Ads" page please go to dubizzle.com, make sure that you are logged into your account, and click on the "My Ads" under your account link that appears at the top of the page.

 

Once you are on the "My Ads" page, you can edit your ad by clicking the "Edit" icon that appears next to each one of your ads.  To delete your ad, you can click on the icon beside the edit icon or you can tick the check box to the left of each ad you wish to delete, and then push the "Delete Selected" button that appears both above and below the listings.

If you need any further assistance you may always contact our support team by visiting http://dubai.dubizzle.com/contact/
