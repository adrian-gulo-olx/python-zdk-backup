How to delete my ad

Please follow these steps to delete your ad on OLX:

  • Go to OLX
  • Click on "Login" and enter your email and password
  • Click on “My ads”. You can find this option at the upper right section of the website
  • Click on the Delete button, located at the right of each of your ads
  • Select the reason why you are deleting your ad and click "Remove Ad"
