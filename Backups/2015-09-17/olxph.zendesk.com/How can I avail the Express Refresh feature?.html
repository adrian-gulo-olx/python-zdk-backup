How can I avail the Express Refresh feature?

To avail the the Express Refresh feature, simply go to your Manage Ads page in the desktop site and select the ad you want to refresh. Upon selecting, you will be directed to the payment page where you can choose your preferred payment option.

Express Refresh can be paid through OLX Gold Credits, Paypal, Credit Card and G-Cash for a minimal fee.
