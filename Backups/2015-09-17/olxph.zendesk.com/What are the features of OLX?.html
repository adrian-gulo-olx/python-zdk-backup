What are the features of OLX?

OLX.ph offers a lot of helpful features that are beneficial for you and your business. These include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Free and easy sign-up
  • Free and easy posting of advertisement
  • Great selection of classified ads under 8 major categories
  • Sponsored Ad feature for better exposure of ads
  • Highlighted Ads
  • Internal currency: OLX Gold
  • Integrated search
  • Integrated bookmark, saved ads, and browsing history

Moving forward, OLX will continue to add and implement features that will improve the way buyers and sellers transact through the online marketplace.
