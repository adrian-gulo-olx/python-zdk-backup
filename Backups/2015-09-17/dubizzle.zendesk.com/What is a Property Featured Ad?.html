What is a Property Featured Ad?

A Featured Ad appears at the top of the search result, with a 25% share of voice in Rentals and 50% in Sale.


A Featured Ad is sold on the basis of a combination of Areas, Bedrooms and Types (villa / apartment).


When you buy a Featured Ad, you can feature any property that matches the combination of Areas, Bedrooms and Type.
