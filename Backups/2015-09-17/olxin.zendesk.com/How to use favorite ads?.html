How to use favorite ads?

Did you find an interesting ad and want to have easy access to it the next time without searching for it in the site? It's easy! Click on the star, which is located at the right of the ad.

         

Are you interested in selected categories or search? Add it to your Favorite list! Click on the star on the top of the list and save the search in Observed searches.

Your observed ads can be found in a tab in the upper right corner of the home page.

For logged in users they will appear even if you are using another system/ browser and even after deleting the files present in cookies in your browser.
