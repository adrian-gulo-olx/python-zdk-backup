How to share my ads?

To share your ad on Facebook, Twitter and Google +, please follow these steps below:

  • Go to OLX
  • Click on Login and enter your email and password
  • Click on My Ads and then click on the ad.
  • Scroll down till you see the Facebook, Twitter or Google + icon
  • Click on any of the icons and Login to your Facebook, Twitter or Google + and share the link
