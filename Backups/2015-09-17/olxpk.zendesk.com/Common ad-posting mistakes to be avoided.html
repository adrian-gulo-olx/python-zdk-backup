Common ad-posting mistakes to be avoided

Content of your ad decides count of buyers. Please ensure your ad is complete with:

  1.   Good and realistic price.
  2.   Catchy title of your item.
  3.   Proper description of the item you want to sell.  
  4.   Proper location since user’s may not want to travel long.
  5.   Real images showing the real condition of the item. Please avoid stock images.
  6.   Proper contact number for buyers to reach out.
  7.   Doesn’t contain words which are used for profanity.
  8.   Doesn’t contain words or products not allowed as per terms of use. Please click here: Posting Rules
