How do I change password for "My Account"

Only registered users require passwords.

If you would like to register and access your ads directly from "My Account," clickÂ here.

If you are a registered user and you wish to change your password, follow these steps:

1. Go to the OLX homepage OLX.com.pk
2. Enter "My Account" using your email address and password.
3. Click on "Settings"
3. Now, click on "Change password."
4. Enter your new password and repeat it again and then click "Change password."
5. Once you set the new password, you will receive an e-mail to reactivate the password.
6. Click on Activate password to complete the process.
7. All set! Your password has been changed.
