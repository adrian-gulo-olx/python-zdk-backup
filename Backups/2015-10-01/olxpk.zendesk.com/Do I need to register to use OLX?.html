Do I need to register to use OLX?

You don't need to register to use OLX. However, registering your account provides you with more flexibility and control on buying and selling. It creates confidence transacting online.

Registering your account with OLX is easy and free!

To register just follow these simple steps:

  1. Go to the OLX
  2. Click "My Account" at the top right corner of the page
  3. Then click on Register
  4. Enter the requested information and click "Create"

We will be sending you a confirmation email to complete your registration. Please check your e-mail (including SPAM) to make sure you receive this e-mail.

Happy Buying and Selling!
