Is it free to post an ad?

Using the OLX (Eg: for adding ads, answering ads) is absolutely free.

We do not charge any commissions related to completed transaction however please note that any business (entrepreneur), as well as a natural person has a duty to clear up with the Office of the Revenue Commissioners from all income, regardless of their source of origin.

This also applies to sales via OLX.com.pk Detailed advice on the content and explanation of the rules connected with it can be found in specialized services on the site.
