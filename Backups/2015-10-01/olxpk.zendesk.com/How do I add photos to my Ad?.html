How do I add photos to my Ad?

Ads with photos are visited on average 3 to 5 times more, so try to add them! How to do it? It's simple.

Option to add photos can be seen below the ad description field. Photos can be added in 2 ways:

1. The first method - the fastest and easiest! Click plus and upload a photo from your computer.

Swap pictures easily and choose the one that has to be displayed as main in the list. Remember! The size of the added images should not exceed 5 MB.

This mechanism is based on Flash technology. If you see the "Download Flash" - it means that your browser does not have this plug-in. Click on the button to install flash-player and follow the instructions, or download the program from the manufacturer:http://get.adobe.com/pl/flashplayer/ 

If, despite the Flash installed and you can't add photos then click on the link "If you're having trouble with flash-player, use the simpler form" and start adding photos by selecting the button "Browse".

2. The second method - 

Directly from your phone! Add photos through a simple click and save option from your smartphone.

Remember! The maximum number of pictures to be added is 8. The maximum size of images is 5 MB and 5000x5000 pixels. Accepted formats are: jpg, jpeg, png.

  1. How do I add photos to an existing ad?

    Do you have an account on OLX.com.pk?

    • Step 1 - Login to your account
    • Step 2 - Go to the tab: "Ads"
    • Step 3 - Go to "Active Ads"
    • Step 4 - Select the ad for which you want to add a picture and click the "edit" (located under the ad title).
    • Step 5 - You will be taken to Edit form of the ad - go to the section "Photos" and add the photos in a most convenient way.

    2. Do not have an account OLX.com.pk?

                    Step 1 - Check the "Manage your ad" e-mail that was sent at the time                 of posting the ad.
                    Step 2 - In the message, find the link to edit ads.

                    Step 3 - Click on the link and follow all the steps described above.
