How Can I Refresh My Free Ads?

Right now, there is no facility yet to refresh your ad on dubizzle

If you wish to push your ad back to the top page, you need to re-post it. If you chose to do this, we just ask that you delete the old ad to prevent duplicates.
Duplicate ad listing can lead to account suspension.

We are continuously doing our best to greatly improve the efficiency of the site's features, it may just take us a little bit of time.
