How Can I Change The Category Of My Free Ads?

Right now, there is no facility yet that will enable you to edit your ad and change the category wherein you originally posted it.

We suggest that you re-post it to the correct category.

If you chose to do this, we just ask that you delete the old ad to prevent duplicates. Duplicate ad listing can lead to account suspension.

We are continuously doing our best to greatly improve the efficiency of the site's features, it may just take us a little bit of time.
