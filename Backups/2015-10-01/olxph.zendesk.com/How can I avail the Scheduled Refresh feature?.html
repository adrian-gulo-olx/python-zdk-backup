How can I avail the Scheduled Refresh feature?

1. The scheduled refresh feature can be accessed through ‘Manage Ads’ page >Select Ad> Feature ad/Boost ad button. The Scheduled Refresh feature will appear in the Multipay VAS page.
 
 
2. Upon reaching the VAS page, you may select the duration of the Scheduled Refresh feature. You can choose between the following options:
    • Automatically refresh every 7 days - 2x (within a 14 day-period)
    • Automatically refresh every 7 days - 4x  (within a 28-day period)

Once you’ve selected an existing refresh schedule,  simply click ‘Buy Feature’.

If the purchase is successful, the Scheduled Refresh feature will no longer appear in the value-added-services page.
