What photos should I use for my item? Can I embed my contact details on them?

You may place your alternative mobile and other contact numbers on your ad photos as long as they do not cover the item or whichever image that depicts what you're promoting in the ad. However, we do advise that your Primary Contact Number is indicated in your profile instead of your ad photos.

What's NOT allowed?

Text that is too large that it covers the item posted

 

What is allowed?

Small text embedded on photos, as long as it doesn't cover the item posted
